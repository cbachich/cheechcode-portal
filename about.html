Personal Information

Education
Christopher was born and raised in Colorado Springs, CO. He graduated high school in 2002 from Sandcreek Highschool and continued his education at the University of Colorado in Colorado Springs (UCCS). He attained a bachelor of science in Computer Science while also minoring in Mathematics and Videogame Development. He later pursued a masters degree in Systems Engineering at the Colorado Technical University.
Current Employment
Christopher is currently a Senior Software Engineer at the Sanborn Map Company, located in Colorado Springs, CO. He develops web based software solutions for the company's wide range of services.
